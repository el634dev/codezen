Using a Trello Board helps keep track of important deadlines, exams and assessments. Send notications to students with ease.

What happens to my data?

Your data that you upload is secure and you can delete at any time. We never share your information, sell your information and use cookies.

How much is CourseZen?

CourseZen is free under 100 students. If you are over 100 students then it's only $10USD per month.

What is CourseZen?

CourseZen is a platform that is similar to DropBox and GitHub but for educators. CourseZen helps keep your course materials in one secure location.
